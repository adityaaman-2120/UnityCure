Kolkata Mega Blood Donation Drive

Blood Drive Help4Life Foundation
Last updated just now

About this Campaign

Join our city-wide effort to ensure safe and sufficient blood supplies. Donors will undergo a quick health screening and receive refreshments and certificates of appreciation. Help save lives and strengthen Kolkata’s emergency response readiness.

Objectives

  • Collect 120+ units of blood to meet regional demand
  • Raise community awareness on regular blood donation
  • Onboard new donors and maintain follow-up registry
What to Expect
  • Simple on-site registration and screening by certified staff
  • Comfortable donation area with hygienic protocols
  • Light refreshments provided post-donation
  • Digital certificate delivered to your registered email
